About Us


At PVS Cashews, we take pride in delivering premium-quality cashew nuts across a wide range of grades at competitive prices.

With a reputation built on trust and excellence, we are one of the most reliable names in the industry, specializing in the export and supply of naturally dried cashew nuts.

Our cashews are carefully dried in hygienic environments to reduce moisture, which not only extends shelf life but also helps preserve their rich taste and protect them from bacterial contamination.

Whether you are a wholesaler, retailer, or end customer, PVS Cashews ensures unmatched quality, freshness, and service — every time.
